Welcome to Indian Railway

Here are some Guidelines given below:

  1. Always verify the Train schedule and any potential delays before heading to the station.

  2. Use online ticketing options to minimize time spent at ticket counters and reduce physical contact.

  3. Arrive at the station with ample time to find your platform and board the train without rushing.

  4. Maintain a safe distance from others while waiting in lines or at platforms.

  5. Listen to announcements regarding boarding procedures and follow any designated boarding order.

  6. Ensure your bags and belongings are secure and within reach to avoid leaving items unattended.

  7. Familarize yourself with emergency exits, procedures, and protocols specific to the train you are on.

  8. In case of emergency,remain calm and follow instructions from train staff.
